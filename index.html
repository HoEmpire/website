Email / Google Scholar / LinkedIn

Research

My research interests focus on state estimation and spatial AI, including probabilistic sensing, localization, mapping, 3D spatial perception.:

1. Efficient Visual-inertial Navigation systems (VINS)
2. Visual-inertial simultaneous localization and mapping (VI-SLAM)
3. 3D environmental perception.
